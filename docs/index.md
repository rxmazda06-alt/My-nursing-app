## About the App
NCLEX Clinical Trainer is an AI-powered nursing exam prep tool for the Next-Generation NCLEX. Built for RN, LVN, and LPT tracks in California.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I access free cases?**
Simply download and open the app — no account required.

**What does Pro include?**
Pro ($34.99/month) unlocks unlimited daily cases, the Performance Dashboard, NCLEX Readiness Predictor, and AI diagnostic tools.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → NCLEX Clinical Trainer → Cancel.

**I found a bug — how do I report it?**
Email nclex.trainer.support@gmail.com with a description of the issue.

## Privacy Policy
This app stores all data locally on your device. No personal data is collected or shared with third parties.
